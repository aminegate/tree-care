What We Do

Professional Arboricultural Services

Our NPTC-certified team is trained to deliver high-quality work, ensuring safety, efficiency, and complete cleanups on every job.

Tree Removal & Felling

Safe dismantling and directional felling of hazardous, dead, or unwanted trees, even in tight and confined garden spaces.

  • ✔ Precision rigging setups
  • ✔ Complete site clearance

Tree Pruning & Crown Reduction

Management of size, weight, and density of tree crowns to promote healthy growth, allow more light, and safeguard structures.

  • ✔ Crown thinning & deadwooding
  • ✔ Work conforming to BS 3998 standards

Hedge Trimming & Maintenance

Precise hedge shaping, height reduction, and regular maintenance for decorative garden hedges and large boundary windbreaks alike.

  • ✔ Crisp, neat, and symmetrical cuts
  • ✔ Green waste fully recycled

Stump Grinding & Removal

Efficient removal of unsightly or trip-hazard tree stumps, grinding them down below ground level to allow replanting or paving.

  • ✔ Advanced stump grinding machinery
  • ✔ Avoids structural upheaval
